Why Locked?

Peace of mind, wherever you are

Wherever you go, with the locked mobile app you’ll be able to receive instant alerts as soon as any of your doors make any motion. Locked comes with remote manual enabling and disabling, allowing you to turn on or off your alarm if your day is not quite going to schedule.

Amazing rates

Can’t afford to break the bank for your home security? Most home security systems with expensive video monitoring comes at a high cost. With Locked’s magnetic motion detection, you pay under 25% of the market price for round the clock security, at just £19.99.

Easy setup

Ditch the ladders, hammers and drills. Locked modules come with EZ-attach functionality. Just attach it to your door.
